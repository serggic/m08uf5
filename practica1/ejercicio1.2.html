Death Note

Death Note (stylized as DEATH NOTE) is a Japanese manga series written by Tsugumi Ohba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata.

Plot

In Tokyo, a disaffected high school student named Light Yagami finds the "Death Note", a mysterious black notebook that can end anyone’s life as long as the writer knows both the target's true name and face. Initially disbelieving, Light quickly considers the renewed possibilities of such an ability at his disposal and annihilates high-profile Japanese criminals then targets international criminals.

Anime

The Death Note anime, directed by Tetsurō Araki and animated by Madhouse, began airing on Nippon TV on October 4, 2006, and finished its run on June 27, 2007, totaling 37 episodes.
